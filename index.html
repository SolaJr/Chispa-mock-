Project Chispa Goals

Reimagine

We aim to provide middles school students with opportunities to imagine how math, science, and coding can lead to creative careers.

Redesign

Students will be taught first hand how to use STEAM concepts in creating video games and films!

Collaberation

Teams will work together to collaborate and build new technology with a student mentor in the Viz program!

We Provide

Along with major entertainment studios, our projectutilizes the principles of STEAM education anddigital world-building as a way to combinecreative freedom and 3D modeling techniqueswith design thinking. Texas A&M students willpartner with 5th-8th grade students inTexas to create imaginary worldsusing game engines.

Whose involved

Media Industry Leaders, Students, Faculty, staff from the viz department and many more!
